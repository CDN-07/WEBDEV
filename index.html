Courses We Offer

Currently, the college offers Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech), Master of Technology (M.Tech), Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Master in Computer Application (MCA) degree courses of SGB Amravati University in various branches.

Undergraduate

1. Mechanical Engineering
2. Electronics and Telecommunications Engineering
3. Civil Engineering
4. Computer Science and Engineering
5. Information Technology
6. Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science
7. Industrial IOT

MISSION

M1 : To achieve the highest standard in technical education through the state-of-the-art pedagogy and enhanced industry Institute linkages.
M2 : To inculcate the culture of research in core and emerging areas.
M3 : To strive for overall development of students so as to nurture ingenious technocrats as well as responsible citizens.
